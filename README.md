## 🧠 Overview
**Meisen Meister** is the official winning entry for the **ODELIA Breast MRI Challenge 2025**, a competition focused on advancing deep learning methods for breast cancer MRI classification.  
Our solution achieved **first place** on the final leaderboard, demonstrating strong generalization and robustness across diverse MRI datasets and acquisition settings.

## 📊 Challenge Context
The [ODELIA Breast MRI Challenge 2025](https://odelia2025.grand-challenge.org/) aimed to benchmark automated methods for breast lesion classification using MRI data.  
Teams were evaluated on a **hidden test set** to ensure generalization and reliability.
